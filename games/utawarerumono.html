Team Attacks & Pairings

In Utawarerumono, you can use Zeal points to execute a Team Attack. Team Attacks can only be used when certain characters are all within 2 squares of an enemy. Each attack has a unique effect, from raw damage to healing to status ailments, or even a mixture of those various elements. They cost 2/3 of the character that initiates's Zeal points, and only 1/2 of the Zeal points of any other character involved.

Keep in mind also that each attack can only be executed by the FIRST character in each bullet point below. If you try it in any other order, the prompt will not show.

Pairings for Team Attacks

  • Hakuoro & Oboro
  • Eruruu & Aruruu
  • Aruruu & Kamyu
  • Oboro & Dorii + Guraa (both must be within range)
  • Dorii & Guraa
  • Dorii & Guraa
  • Benawi & Kurou
  • Kurou & Oboro
  • Karura & Touka
  • Touka & Aruruu
  • Touka, Aruruu, Gachyatara
  • Urutorii & Karura
  • Kamyu & Urutorii

From this, we can see that the characters that will most commonly be able to be used for team attacks are:

  1. Aruruu (4 pairings)
  2. Oboro, Dorii, & Guraa (3 pairings)
  3. Kurou, Karura, & Urutorii (2 pairings)

You may want to think about this ranking if you want Team Attacks to be your primary strategy for winning battles.
