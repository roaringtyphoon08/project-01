Minerals

Mining is a big part of Spectrobes as minerals will increase your Spectrobe's stats. In order to be able to obtain minerals, you need a Spectrobe with a small diamond in their search properties menu (Press X > Status > Touch Spectrobe with touch screen).

Types of Minerals

Basic Minerals

"Basic" minerals include the following minerals:

  • Triangle Shaped (Attack)
  • Rectangle Shaped (Defense)
  • Cresent Shaped (HP)

Each of these minerals will have a certain color. The color corresponds with the number your Spectrobe's stats will increase by when given to them. In order from least valuable to most valuable, it goes:

  1. Yellow (+8)
  2. Green (+16)
  3. Pink (+24)
  4. Purple (+32)

In game, these are called C, B, A, and A+ minerals respectively.
